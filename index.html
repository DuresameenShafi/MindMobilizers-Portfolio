Introducing the revolutionary Brain-Controlled Wheelchair, a cutting-edge mobility solution designed to enhance accessibility and independence for individuals with mobility impairments. This state-of-the-art wheelchair combines advanced neuroscience technology with intuitive controls, offering users unprecedented freedom and ease of movement. Utilizing brain-computer interface (BCI) technology, users can effortlessly navigate the wheelchair simply by directing their thoughts, eliminating the need for manual input. Moreover, this innovative wheelchair features seamless integration with mobile devices via Bluetooth connectivity, allowing users to control their chair conveniently from their smartphones or tablets.
